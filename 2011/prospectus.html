--- title: Sponsorship Prospectus layout: '2011' ---

MWRC is a two day single-track conference on March 17-18, 2011 in Salt Lake City, UT. Admission is expected to be 250-300 attendees. We provide opportunities to for companies to market to and interact with attendees both at the conference and through online videos.

Download the PDF

Gold Sponsorship - $5,000

  • Prominent position on online videos
  • Company name, logo, and 200 word description on website
  • Company logo in Program Guide
  • Two pages in Conference Magazine
  • Sponsor designation in marketing
  • 8 Conference passes
  • Attendee Handouts

Silver Sponsorships - $2,000

  • Secondary position on online videos
  • Company name, logo, and 100 word description on website
  • Company logo in Program Guide
  • One page in Conference Magazine
  • Sponsor designation in marketing
  • 4 Conference passes
  • Attendee Handouts

Bronze Sponsorships - $500

  • Position at end of online videos
  • Company name, logo, and 50 word description on website
  • Company logo in Program Guide
  • 1/3 page in Conference Magazine
  • Sponsor designation in marketing
  • 1 Conference pass

All magazine advertisements are due one week after the conference. Failure to provide advertisement will forfeit your position.

If you are interested in sponsoring the conference, please contact:
MountainWest Ruby, LLC
sponsorship@mtnwestrubyconf.org

We understand that some organizations have restrictions on making direct cash contributions to some conferences or business entities. If you are interested in sponsorship, but are unable to directly contribute funds to MountainWest Ruby, LLC, we would like to explore opportunities for sponsorship. Please contact us and we will work hard to find a way for you to help support the conference.
