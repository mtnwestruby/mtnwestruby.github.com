The MountainWest family of conferences continues to grow. In 2014 we will hold a three conference event over five days covering JavaScript, DevOps, and Ruby in Salt Lake City. JavaScript will be presented on March 17-18, DevOps on March 19, and Ruby on March 20-21. We expect 300 attendees per day, and as much as 500 unique attendees across all three conferences.

Platinum Ruby Sponsorship - $8,000

The premier sponsor for both days of MountainWest RubyConf. This sponsorship level includes the most prominent logo placement during Ruby conference events and on Ruby conference videos.

  • Headline sponsor for Ruby conference sessions
  • Preeminent logo position on Ruby conference banners and videos
  • Gold Sponsorship for JavaScript and Devops conferences
  • Company name, logo, and 200 word description on website
  • Sponsor designation in marketing
  • Marketing material in attendee swag bag
  • 4 passes to all conferences
  • 4 additional passes to Ruby conference

Gold Sponsorship - $5,000 (Limit 6)

This sponsorship level includes prominent logo placement during conference events and on conference videos.

  • Sponsor designation for Ruby, JavaScript, and DevOps conferences
  • Prominent logo position on all conference banners and videos
  • Company name, logo, and 100 word description on conference websites
  • Sponsor designation in marketing
  • Marketing material in attendee swag bag
  • 4 passes to all conferences

Outside Events

Sponsor are able to host activities during the conference week. These activities will be added to the offical schedule, as long as they do not conflict with conference proceedings.

Scholarships

Sponsors are encouraged to donate unused tickets to the MountainWest HackWeek scholarship program. Tickets will be made available to those wanting to attend but without financial means. Sponsors donating tickets to the MountainWest HackWeek scholarship program will be displayed on the scholarship section of the site.
