Opening Keynote on Merb and Rubinius Announced!

We are very pleased to announce that Ezra Zygmuntowicz and Evan Phoenix will give our opening keynote entitled "Strengthening the Ruby Ecosystem". We have a full description of the keynote in our speakers section.

Ezra is the co-founder of Engine Yard, creater of Merb, and author of the upcoming Rails Deployment book. Evan is the project manager for the Rubinius project and works at Engine Yard.

Jim Weirich Keynote Announced!

We are very pleased to announce that Jim Weirich is one of our keynote speakers. Jim will present "Shaving with Ockham". We have more details of his keynote in our speakers section.

Jim Weirich is the Chief Scientist for EdgeCase LLC, a Rails development firm located in Columbus Ohio. Jim has over twenty-five years of experience in software development. He has worked with real-time data systems for testing jet engines, networking software for information systems, and image processing software for the financial industry. Jim is active in the Ruby community and has contributed to several Ruby projects, including the Rake build system and the RubyGems package software.
