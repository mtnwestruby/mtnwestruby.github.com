MountainWest RubyConf is the longest running regional Ruby conference. We just can't succeed without our amazing sponsors. Each year we host Rubyists from all over the world for two days of single-track goodness. Our attendees appreciate the shared experience and sense of community the conference fosters, and our sponsors are a vital part of making it happen.

Sponsoring MWRC will put your organization in front of the expected 350+ professional developers, hobbiest hackers, designers, and managers that will be attending, as well as tens of thousands more that will view the recordings online. Please consider lending your support with one of the following sponsorship packages.

Platinum Sponsorship - $5,000 (Limit 1)

The premier sponsor for MountainWest RubyConf 2015. This sponsorship level includes the most prominent placement among all the sponsors.

  • Headline sponsor, top-billing
  • Preeminent logo position on conference banners and videos
  • Premier location for table at conference
  • Company name, logo, and 200 word description on website
  • Sponsor designation in marketing
  • Marketing material on registration table
  • Four tickets to attend the conference

Gold Sponsorship - $2,500 (Limit 8)

This sponsorship level includes prominent logo placement during conference events and on conference videos.

  • Prominent logo position on conference banners and videos
  • Available location for table at conference
  • Company name, logo, and 100 word description on website
  • Sponsor designation in marketing
  • Marketing material on registration table
  • Two tickets to attend the conference

Silver Sponsorship - $1,000

This sponsorship level includes logo placement during conference events and on conference videos.

  • Logo on conference banners and videos
  • Company name, logo, and 50 word description on website
  • Sponsor designation in marketing
  • One ticket to attend the conference

Scholarships

Sponsors are encouraged to donate unused tickets to the MountainWest RubyConf scholarship program. Tickets will be made available to those wanting to attend but without financial means. Sponsors donating tickets to the scholarship program will be displayed on the scholarship section of the site.
