Instructure 5K Fun Run

Come join us for the sixth annual MountainWest RubyConf 5K, sponsored by Instructure. Runners and walkers of all abilities are welcome!

When & Where

Tuesday, March 10, 2015 at 7:00 A.M. (we’ll be back before the conference starts).

Liberty Park, located at 600 E 1300 S. We’ll be meeting at the southeast corner.

Prizes

There will be sweet tech running shirts for all participants. We will also have prizes, but they will be awarded to participants via random drawing at the event, rather than to the fastest runners. Many thanks to Instructure for sponsoring the race and prizes this year!

How much?

It’s free! But people will need rides and showers after the event.

  • Locals can offer a ride!
  • Visitors can offer the use of hotel showers! We’ll coordinate on Thursday. If you’re on Twitter, we’ll be using the #mwrc5k hashtag.
