Engine Yard Hackfest Returns!

Engine Yard is back again and hosting their Hackfest at the Hilton on the evening of Thursday, March 11. If you haven't ever been to their Hackfest then we encourage you to be a responsible MWRC attendee and attend. Bring a project to work on, or be prepared to help someone else work on something while you learn more about a new library or framework.

Update: Be sure to join the Hackfest event on Facebook!

Need a place to stay?

Are you attending MountainWest RubyConf and coming from out of town? We've got an unfinished basement you can crash on, or, if you like amenities like a bed and electricity, you can stay at one of the local hotels.

There are plenty of hotels within walking distance of the conference venue. You can see them here. James Britt also has a GitHub repo with more information for visitors from out of town. If you have any questions you can direct them to our twitter account, or you can send an email to mike@mtnwestrubyconf.org
